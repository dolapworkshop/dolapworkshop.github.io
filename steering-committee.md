This page presents the composition of the Steering Committee (SC) of DOLAP.

The SC is the organ with the ultimate decision authority for DOLAP, with its composition, obligations, and responsibilities being detailed in the [Bylaws](policies.md). The SC is presided by a Chair and a Vice Chair.

# Steering Committee composition

## Chairs

The mandate of the current SC Chairs is from May 2026 to April 2029.

- **[Chair of the SC]** Panos Vassiliadis, *University of Ioannina (Greece)*
- **[Vice Chair of the SC]** Enrico Gallinucci, *University of Bologna (Italy)*
